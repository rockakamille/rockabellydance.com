    • Kamille continuously pushes toward unabashed fulfillment. Passion and an unexpected sense of humor drive her performances. She uses music of all types to tap in to the audience's own memories to stir their hearts, and adds to it a deep knowledge of history and ethnic movement to excite their minds. This lead her to become the fearless leader of RockaBelly in 2004. She is very proud of RockaBelly's apprentice troupe, The Playthings, which began in 2012. She is happy to be here, and happy to have conquered breast cancer.
